ABOUT HOST

Welcome to Stay Wild Home stay

Stay Wild was established in June 2016 by Mr. K R Ananth Kumar is an agriculturist and is the proprietor. The proprietor along with his family and the homestay is managed and maintained by his son Mohan(Ranju).

Stay Wild Home stay gives a quality of service and exceptional comfort !!

The windows are overlooking the beautiful sky touching hills, Coffee estate, waterfalls, Wide play ground, Garden, and the mesmerizing fog feels you relaxed.
In our homestay you can taste the traditional coorg breakfast (place order the day before) such as Akki rotti, kadambittu(Rice balls), Neer Dose, Idminittu and delicious dinner at night. Lunch is provided on request at extra cost. It also offers other facilities like chess, carom. There is a wide ground in front of the gate there you can play cricket, football, kabaddi, shuttle badminton and also go for a walk in a fresh breeze. It has huge parking space.
In Stay Wild you can indulge and enjoy in fire camp and taste delicious starters Hot & Spicy Bar-B-Que (additional cost - place ordered the day before) with homemade wines(additional cost) in the night by watching the millions of stars in the sky creates the mood to dance around the warm flame.
